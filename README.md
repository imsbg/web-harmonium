# 🎹 Web Harmonium Pro

**Web Harmonium Pro** is a high-quality, fully responsive virtual Indian Harmonium playable directly in your web browser. Whether you are practicing classical Indian music, testing out Swarams, or just having fun, this app provides real studio sounds with zero latency.

## ✨ Features

- **🎵 Authentic Sounds:** Powered by high-quality studio-recorded harmonium samples (`.wav`).
- **📱 Mobile Optimized:** Auto-prompts for full-screen landscape mode on mobile devices with flawless, bug-free multi-touch support.
- **🎼 Custom Notation Engine:** Write or paste standard notes (e.g., `S R G M P D N S2`) and watch the harmonium play them automatically with adjustable speeds.
- **🎛️ Studio Controls:** Built-in Reverb toggle, Volume slider, Octave shifter (Saptak), and Transpose (Mool Swar).
- **💻 Desktop Ready:** Play seamlessly using your standard QWERTY keyboard or mouse.
- **🚀 Zero Dependencies:** Built entirely using HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, and the Web Audio API.

## 🎮 How to Play

### On Desktop:
- Use your mouse to click the keys.
- Or use your computer keyboard! The middle row (`Q` to `]`) acts as the white keys, and the top number row (`1` to `=`) acts as the black keys.

### On Mobile:
- Simply rotate your device to landscape mode when prompted, enter full screen, and use your fingers to play multi-touch chords naturally.

### Autoplay Notations:
1. Scroll down to the notation text box.
2. Type your sequence using standard English keys or Swarams (e.g., `S R G M P D N S2`).
3. Adjust the speed using the slider and click **Play**.
